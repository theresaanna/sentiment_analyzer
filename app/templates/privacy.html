1. Introduction

Welcome to VibeCheckAI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://vibecheckai.pro and use our AI-powered sentiment analysis services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: Name, email address, through our third-party authentication integrations such as Google.
  • Payment Information: Billing details and payment method information, processed securely through third-party payment processors
  • Content Data: Text, comments, posts, or other content you upload for sentiment analysis
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting our support team

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service, features used, time spent, and frequency of use
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience (see Cookie Policy section)

2.3 Third-Party Information

  • Information from social media platforms or other services you choose to connect with our service
  • Analytics data from third-party analytics providers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our sentiment analysis services
  • Process your text content through our AI models to generate insights
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process payments and billing
  • Communicate with you about service updates, support, and promotional offers
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our service
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI Processing and Data Handling

4.1 Sentiment Analysis Processing

  • Your uploaded content is processed by our AI models to generate sentiment insights
  • We temporarily store your content during processing and cache it for up to 24 hours following.
  • Processed content may be used in aggregate (anonymized) form to improve our AI models
  • We do not manually review your content unless required for technical support or legal compliance

4.2 Data Retention

  • Content uploaded for analysis: Deleted after processing unless you choose to save results
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after closure
  • Usage analytics: Retained in anonymized form for service improvement

5.3 With Your Consent
